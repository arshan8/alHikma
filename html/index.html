Our Mission

We are on the Mission to promote inclusive development by educating and skilling underprivileged children through easy access to quality education. This is to achieve rapid educational transformation in the weaker sections of society who usually cannot afford the expenses of educating their children. We aim at providing quality education in line with the CBSE & ISC Curriculum.

Since the students come from the underprivileged and deprived section of society, they are also malnourished and unkempt; hence Al Hikmah also aims at improving their health and hygiene by providing healthy food with a balanced diet and self-care training. These children and their families cannot even afford basic food, clothing, and other necessities. At Al Hikmah Institute, our mission is to provide good food, clothing, medical facilities, self-care and self-defense training, and other essential facilities that bring holistic development within them.

We aim to work on social problems such as child marriage, sexual violence, lack of awareness about menstrual hygiene and child labour, drug addiction, and exposure to drug environments faced by children of marginalized communities.

para 1

Initially what was merely a vision to us—and a frivolous notion for others—Al Hikmah Institute started with just 44 orphan kids in a 1,269-square-foot apartment. After relentless toiling, land was taken on lease and that commitment has transpired into a 16,552.8-square-foot premises that imparts quality education and holistic nourishment of both body and mind to more than 350 destitute and helpless children.

It is after two years of rigorous and selfless hard work that we now seek to purchase two acres of land upon which we have laid the foundation of a residential school—one that will comprise a full school building and related facilities.
